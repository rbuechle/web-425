Who was Lois Lowry?

Lois Lowry was the middle child of three and moved all over due to her family being a military family. She married at the young age of 19 to a gentleman in the Navy and then had grew their family by having four children. She began to write when she later returned for her graduate degree. She dreamed of writing since she was a child and finally got to start. The books she wrote varies in content and style but are all remarkable works of art. Lois has gone on to write many books that have won prizes such as the Newbery Medal.
