Trans-Siberian Orchestra - About

In 1996 Paul O’Neill formed the Trans-Siberian Orchestra. After forming he then recruited several friends of his Robert Kinkel and Jon Oliva to be apart of his core writing team. Paul O’Neill had amazing talent and worked for years leading up to this working as a writer and producer in the music industry. His list of famous artists that he worked with was several miles long such as people like Madonna, Sting and Aerosmith. Sadly, though his life was cut short when he passed away in April of 2017.
